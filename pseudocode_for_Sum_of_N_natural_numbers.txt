12. Assignment: Sum of N Natural Numbers
take a positive integer N as input and calculate the sum of the first N natural numbers using a loop. 
Output the result.

ans) Pseudocode for Finding sum of natural numbers
----------------------------------------------------------

Begin
Declare n,sum=0,i=1;
Display "Enter the n value;
input n;
while(i<=n) THEN
	sum=sum+i;
	i=i+1;
End while
output sum;
End
