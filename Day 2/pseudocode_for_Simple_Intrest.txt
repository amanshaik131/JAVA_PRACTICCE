4Assignment: .Simple Interest:
take the principal amount, rate of interest, and time in years as input and compute the simple interest. 
Output the result.

Pseudocode for finding Simple Intrest:
------------------------------------------
Begin
Declare p,t,r,si;
Display "Enter the values of p,t,r";
input p,t,r;
si=(p*t*r)/100;
output si;
End
