3. Find the area and peremeter of circle, rectangle and square

Pseudocode for finding Area :
--------------------------------
Begin
Declare radius,length,breadth,side,area,peri;
Display "Enter the radius of Circle ";
input radius
area=3.14*radius*radius;
peri=2*3.14*radius
output area,peri
Display "Enter the length and breadth of Rectangle ";
input length,breadth;
area=length*breadth;
peri=2*(length+breadth);
output area,peri
Display "Enter the side of a Square "
input side;
area=side*side;
peri=4*side;
output area,peri;
End
