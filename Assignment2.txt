2.Assignment: Average and Percentage:  
take the marks of a student in five subjects as input and compute the average and percentage. 
Output both the average and percentage.

Algorithm:
------------

Step 1 : Start
Step 2 : Declare the five variables  marks1,marks2,marks3,marks4,marks5,avg,percent
Step 3 : Read the values of marks
Step 4 : now calculate the avg doing of sum of marks /number of subjects such as 
		avg=marks1+marks2+marks3+marks4+marks5/no of subjects and store the 
		result in avg.  
Step 5 : now perform percent=avg/100*100;
Step 6 : print avg and percent
Step 7 : Stop
